What is the voting mechanism?

Community members can cast their vote to support the token they believe deserves more attention. Every eligible wallet can participate, helping create a transparent ranking based entirely on community activity.

How does the community voting work?

Voting is open for a limited period. During this time, participants submit their vote, and the leaderboard updates to reflect overall community support. The process is designed to be fair, simple, and transparent.

What happens when voting ends?

Once the voting period is over, the final results are calculated and published. The community can view the completed ranking and see which token received the strongest overall support.
